How the Standard Evolves

01 / Proposal

Community Input

Practitioners, researchers, and advisors submit revision proposals. All submissions are reviewed against evidence from real organisations.

02 / Review

Independent Panel

A rotating panel of domain experts validates proposals for rigour, applicability, and consistency with the BASE-100 framework principles.

03 / Release

Versioned Revision

Approved changes are published as a dated revision. Previous versions remain archived and accessible — the record is never erased.
