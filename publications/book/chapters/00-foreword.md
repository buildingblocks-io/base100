# Foreword

## Why this book exists

BASE-100 is not opinion dressed as methodology. It is an **open standard**: a shared way to see a company as a system, to measure maturity in plain language, and to audit what matters before ambition outruns execution.

This volume brings together the narrative that motivates the standard and the **domain reference** that makes it operational. Use the front matter to align on intent; use Part II when you need definitions, levels, and audit language you can repeat without drift. A short **Afterword** closes the book after the domains.

The Institute publishes revisions on a fixed cadence so the standard stays honest with how real companies operate. Your edition matches the **revision** on the cover, in the page footers, and in the **Copyright, edition, and revisions** chapter in this Part I.

Welcome to the map.
