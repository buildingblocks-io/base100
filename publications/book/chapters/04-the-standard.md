# The Standard

## The 10 by 10 Matrix

The standard is a bi-dimensional matrix used to normalise organisational complexity.

- **X-axis.** The 10 universal domains.
- **Y-axis.** The 10 levels of maturity.

## The 10 Domains

Every recurring business problem belongs in one of these ten boxes:

1. Leadership and Direction
2. Product and Innovation
3. Brand and Market
4. Revenue and Sales
5. Customer Success and Retention
6. Talent and Culture
7. Operations and Delivery
8. Technology and Infrastructure
9. Finance and Risk
10. Assets and IP

The power of the model is not only that it names these domains. It places them on one shared operating map.
