# How to Read This Book

This volume has two parts.

The opening chapters explain the logic behind the standard in editorial form. They are meant to be read straight through.

The second part is the working standard itself. Each domain opens with a direction section and then moves through ten maturity levels. Each level defines what must be true, why it matters, which evidence proves it, and what signals indicate failure.

## Review Frequency

Where this book lists a review frequency, the number means **times per year**.

- 1 = annual
- 2 = twice per year
- 4 = quarterly
- 12 = monthly
- 52 = weekly
- 250 = daily working rhythm

The point of the frequency field is simple: a maturity claim only matters if it remains true in operation.
